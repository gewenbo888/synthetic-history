# Synthetic History · 合成历史

Synthetic History generates alternate timelines from six well-studied divergence points (Tang continuance, no Industrial Revolution outside Britain, no Black Death, Cuban Missile Crisis goes hot, no public internet by 2000, no GPT moment in 2022) × three trajectories each (convergent / divergent / ruined) = 18 templated alternates with explicit "honest meta-read" on what the counterfactual does and does not tell us. Bilingual (EN / 中文), dark/light, timeline-branch brand (Spectral italic display + Karla body + JetBrains Mono on `#0a0814` with chrono-violet primary `#a87aff` plus past-gold, counter-cyan, branch-pink codes; Noto Serif SC for Chinese). Hero: animated SVG single timeline branching into three coloured alternates at a single divergence node. Three modules: S1 Timeline Generator (6 × 3 = 18 templated alternates with immediate effect + 50-year arc + honest meta-read of what counterfactual reasoning actually shows), S2 Reader (5 multi-paragraph canned probes — useful-divergence properties, what-counterfactual-tells-us, why-uncomfortable-honesty, what-the-genre-sells-vs-discipline, using-this-generator-well — plus free-text heuristic), S3 Guardrails (no prediction in alt-history clothes, no ethnic flattering, no "great man" determinism, no moral arbitrage, no infinite-AI-generation slop).
